What Services I Provide

Six photography specialities, plus editing and printing, so your story is handled from the shutter click to the finished frame.

Nature Photography

Landscapes, forests and the outdoors, shot around Kyoto's changing seasons.

Portrait Sessions

Individual and family portraits, in-studio or on location, lit to flatter.

Wedding Photography

Full-day coverage of the ceremony, portraits and the moments in between.

Street Photography

Candid, unscripted sets from the city — editorial-style and documentary.

Travel Photography

On-location shoots for trips, brands and publications, packed light.

Wildlife Photography

Patient, long-lens sessions to document animals in their own habitat.

Photo Editing & Retouching

Colour grading, skin retouching and careful, restrained post-production.

Fine Art Prints

Museum-grade prints and albums, so your favourites leave the screen.
